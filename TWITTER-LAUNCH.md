## Legal Safety Notes

✅ **What we did right:**
- Used "alternative" and "for multi-LLM development" (descriptive, not trademark infringement)
- Focused on OUR features (multi-model support, cost savings, open source)
- No claims of affiliation or endorsement
- No use of their logos or trademarks
- Comparative advertising is legally protected (15 U.S.C. § 1125(a))

✅ **What we avoided:**
- "Better than Claude Code" (subjective comparison)
- "Claude Code replacement" (too aggressive)
- Using their branding
- Claiming any affiliation
- Negative comparisons

✅ **Why this is safe:**
- We're describing what we ARE (multi-LLM CLI)
- We're being truthful about features
- We have takedown policy in README
- Zero Anthropic code
- Just calling public REST APIs
