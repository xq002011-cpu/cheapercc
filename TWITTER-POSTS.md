## 🛡️ Legal Safety Guidelines

### ✅ Safe Phrases (Use These)
- "Multi-LLM CLI"
- "Switch models anytime"
- "No vendor lock-in"
- "Your choice of AI"
- "Developer flexibility"
- "Cost control"
- "Open source alternative"

### ⚠️ Avoid (Too Aggressive)
- "Better than X"
- "Replace X"
- "X killer"
- Direct negative comparisons
- Claims of affiliation

### ✅ Comparative Language (Safe When True)
- "Alternative to..."
- "Cheaper than..." (if factually true)
- Feature comparisons (when factual)
- "Works with X, Y, Z..." (neutral listing)
